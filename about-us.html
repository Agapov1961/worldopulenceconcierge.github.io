PRIVACY POLICY

This privacy notice aims to give you information on how WORLD OPULENCE CONCIERGE collects and processes your personal data through your use of this website or our services, including any data you may provide through this website when you sign up to our newsletter, purchase a product or service or take part in a competition. This website is not intended for children.

We reserve the right to transfer Personal Information in the event that we merge with or are acquired by a third party. We also may disclose your Personal Information for any other purpose permitted by law or to which you consent.

At World Opulence Concierge, we are committed to protecting the privacy of our clients.

1. Collection of Personal Information

We may collect personal information such as names, contact details, and billing information for the purpose of providing concierge services.

2. Use of Personal Information

We may use the personal information collected to:

  • Facilitate the requested concierge services
  • Communicate with clients regarding their requests
  • Process billing and payment transactions
  • Improve our services and personalize the client experience

3. Sharing of Personal Information

We understand the importance of keeping personal information private and will not disclose or share it with any third parties, except:

  • When required by law or governmental authorities
  • To prevent or investigate potential illegal activities
  • With trusted service providers who assist us in operating our business

4. Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect personal information from unauthorized access or disclosure.

5. Update and Access to Personal Information

Clients can request to update or access their personal information by contacting us directly. We will make efforts to accommodate such requests within a reasonable timeframe.

6. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to modify or update this privacy policy at any time. Clients will be notified of any changes through email or by posting a notice on our website.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our privacy policy, please contact us at privacy@wo-concierge.com
